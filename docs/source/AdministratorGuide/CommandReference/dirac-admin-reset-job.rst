=====================
dirac-admin-reset-job
=====================

Reset a job or list of jobs in the WMS

Usage::

  dirac-admin-reset-job [option|cfgfile] ... JobID ...

Arguments::

  JobID:    DIRAC ID of the Job

Example::

  $ dirac-admin-reset-job 1848
  Reset Job 1848
